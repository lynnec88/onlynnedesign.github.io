Accessibility Statement

Onlynne Design is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including Deaf and disabled communities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Measures to Support Accessibility

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It outlines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA.

Onlynne Design takes the following measures to ensure accessibility:

  • Include accessibility and Deaf user inclusion as part of our mission statement.
  • Integrate accessibility into our design, development, and procurement practices.
  • Design with Deaf, Hard-of-Hearing, Blind, and other disabled users in mind from the start.
  • Provide continual accessibility training and resources for our team.
  • Collaborate with disabled users, including Deaf creatives, in testing and feedback.

Conformance Status

Onlynne Design is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA. This means some parts of the content may not fully meet all accessibility standards yet, but we are actively working to improve.

Compatibility with Browsers and Assistive Technology

Onlynne Design is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Google Chrome with built-in accessibility tools on Windows 10+
  • Safari with built-in VoiceOver and accessibility tools on macOS
  • Screen readers and keyboard navigation tools
  • Visual alternatives for audio content and captions for video

The website is not compatible with:

  • Browsers older than 3 major versions
  • Mobile operating systems older than 5 years

Technical Specifications

Accessibility of the Onlynne Design website relies on the following technologies to work with browsers and assistive technologies:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

Limitations and Alternatives

Despite our best efforts, there may still be some accessibility limitations. We are actively identifying and fixing them.

If you experience any issues, please reach out at hello@onlynne.com, and we will work to provide alternatives or solutions.

Assessment Approach

Onlynne Design assessed the accessibility of this website by the following methods:

  • Internal accessibility review
  • Deaf user testing (where applicable)
  • Accessibility statement approved by internal leadership

This statement was formally approved by Onlynne Design in 2025.

Formal Complaints

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days and resolve issues within 10 business days. If you are dissatisfied with our response, you have the right to escalate your complaint to a national authority or accessibility advocacy group.
